# Closing the Gap Between Design and Production

For decades, the standard workflow in manufacturing was heavily segmented. Designers worked in CAD (Computer-Aided Design), exported flat files, and handed them off to production engineers. 

Today, this segmented workflow is no longer acceptable. The industry demands **agile manufacturing**, which requires real-time integration between 3D designs, Bills of Materials (BOMs), and the factory floor.

## The Problem with Siloed Systems

When CAD and ERP exist in silos, the risks multiply:
- **Data Duplication:** Engineers waste time manually typing BOMs into the ERP.
- **Version Control Issues:** Using outdated drawings leads to expensive manufacturing errors on the shop floor.
- **Slow Iteration:** Updating a design takes weeks instead of days because the feedback loop is broken.

## Our Approach at I.E TECH

We don't just sell software. We build the bridges between your existing tools. By utilizing powerful APIs (like Autodesk Forge or SolidWorks PDM API), we pull geometric data, metadata, and BOMs directly into the core ERP system we build for you.

When an engineer hits "Save" on a part assembly, the entire supply chain knows what materials to order immediately. That is true operational efficiency.
